## 🚀 About

Lanexor is a new messenger designed for community communication and collaboration.  
It brings together everything that matters most: text chats, voice channels, file sharing, roles, and access control.  

The main goal is to provide a fast and reliable tool that stays available regardless of restrictions or blocks.

---

## 🎯 Who is it for

- 🕹 Gaming communities  
- 👨‍💻 Project teams  
- 📝 Work groups  
- 🤝 Friends who need a stable and convenient messenger  

---

## ✨ Our principles

- 🔹 **Ease of use** — intuitive from the very first click  
- ⚡ **Performance** — lightweight interface without clutter  
- 🔒 **Privacy** — data protection and access control  
- 🛡 **Resilience** — always available, even under restrictions  

---

## 🌍 Why Lanexor?

Lanexor is an alternative to traditional messengers,  
with its own unique style, architecture, and vision for the future.  

We are building a platform that can become a home for communities, projects, and friends.
