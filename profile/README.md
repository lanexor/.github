## 🚀 About

**Lanexor** is a next generation messenger built for community communication and teamwork.
It unites everything that matters: text chats, voice channels, file exchange, roles, and precise access control.

Its mission is simple: provide a **fast, stable, and reliable** tool that stays available even under tough network restrictions.

---

## 🎯 Who is it for

* 🕹 Gaming communities
* 👨‍💻 Developer and project teams
* 📝 Work and study groups
* 🤝 Friends who need a dependable, convenient messenger

---

## ✨ Core Principles

* 🔹 **Simplicity** - intuitive from the very first interaction
* ⚡ **High performance** - clean, lightweight interface
* 🔒 **Privacy first** - strict data protection and role-based access
* 🛡 **Resilience** - works even when others fail

## 🌍 Why Lanexor?

Lanexor is not just another messenger.
It introduces a fresh ecosystem with its own style, powerful architecture, and a long term vision.

We are building a platform that can become a digital home for communities, teams, and friends around the world.
